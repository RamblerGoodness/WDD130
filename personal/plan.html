Overview

Purpose

I have been playing around with AI art generation so i want to use this site to show off what i've made so far. I also want to use this site to educate people about how AI art works. I will also use this site to show off my art made by AI and the technics I've learned.

Audience

Any one who might be interested in AI generated art. My target is people who are interested as a hobby. I wont be able to show them how to make AI art in a professional setting or use. This is more to show off and get people interested in AI art.
To best cater to the intended audience I should:
Give a brief explanation to AI art and what kind of AI model I use.
Give lots of examples of art that I generated and the prompts i used.
Show people how they can get started using AI to make there own art.
